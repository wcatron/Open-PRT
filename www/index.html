About

Open source documentation and protocols for a scalable Personal Rapid Transit (PRT, aka: PodCar) system.

Vision

To create a standard for communciation between riders, podcars and decenteralized servers to maintain a PRT system. Our inspiration comes from growth of the internet due to standards, openness and healthy capitalism. We believe that any successful system will come about through a community effort and not by a single governing body, company or even a select few.

Who is Open PRT?

Open PRT is an advocate for PRT and seeks to offer a solution through standardization and partnerships with industry leaders and interested parties. We are focused on creating a standard for PRT installations that will allow for growth and innovation for the next century. To be clear there currently is not a singular Open PRT design but we are seeking interested partners to contribute to creating a design that could ultimately be implemented in Charleston and around the world.
